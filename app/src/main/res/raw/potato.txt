The potato (also referred to as tater,tattie, and spud) is a root vegetable that is part of the plant Solanum tuberosum. This perennial is part of the family Solanaceae or the nightshades. In many contexts, the word potato refers to the edible starchy tuber portion of the plant, but it can also refer to the plant itself,which is native to the Americas.Following millennia of selective breeding, there are now over 1,000 different types of potatoes.
